Over the past few months you would have seen increased activity at the club, such as additional NSO training, new member training, long gun side matches, the 5x5 classifier on more regular basis and a few more. This is done to broaden the offering to the club members. Additionally, there is an ongoing interaction with potential ranges that may host us in the future.

To make this a sustainable effort, and assist growing the club activities more (especially with interaction with ranges), the club is requiring assistance from the members. We have received some assistance on an ongoing basis, but more structured assistance will be required.
