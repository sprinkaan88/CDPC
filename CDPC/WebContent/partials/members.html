Renewal

Membership of IDPA, SADPA and a Club of your choice is compulsory.
The following needs to be completed:
  • IDPA membership - $40
  • SADPA membership renewal form, proof of IDPA membership (bottom of account page) and proof of payment to SADPA (R265), emailed to SADPA)
  • CDPC renewal form and proof of payment (R400) emailed to admin@cdpc.co.za
  • New - Family membership fee of R600. This includes two adults and children under the age of 18

New Members

Membership of IDPA, SADPA and a Club of your choice is compulsory.
The following needs to be completed:
  • IDPA membership - $40
  • SADPA membership application form, proof of IDPA membership (bottom of account page) and proof of payment to SADPA (R265), emailed to SADPA)
  • CDPC application form and proof of payment (R550) emailed to admin@cdpc.co.za
